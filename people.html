Principal Investigator

Gizem Dilber Acar, Ph.D.

Prof. Gizem D. Acar is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Stevens Institute of Technology. She earned her B.S. and M.S. degrees from Bogazici University in 2010 and 2012, respectively. She received her Ph.D. from Michigan State University in 2017, and worked as a postdoctoral research associate at the University of Maryland until 2020. Her research interests include dynamics and vibrations with a focus on wind turbines, systems with noise, wave propagation in solids and metamaterials, and granular media. email: gizem.acar@stevens.edu

Courses taught: E 120, ENGR 211 ME 358, ME 631, and ME 633

Mahdi Yari

Mahdi is a Ph.D. student at the Stevens Institute of Technology with a focus on the implementation of meta-structures for vibration attenuation purposes. He received both his M.Sc. and B.Sc. in Shiraz University, Iran where he worked on designing novel lattice structures. His research interests also lie in the field of mechanical metamaterials and periodic structures along with their real-life applications. email: myari@stevens.edu
